IELTS Listening Part 1: Form Completion

Level 5.5 Strategy Practice: Prediction • Numbers • Spellings

Step 1: Before Listening - Predict the Answer Type

Look at each gap and decide what kind of answer you need.

Numbers

Dates, years, prices, phone numbers

Spellings

Names, streets, uncommon words

Vocabulary

Activities, occupations, goals

Step 4: Key Listening Strategies

For Numbers:

  • Listen for dates, prices, years
  • Watch for corrections
  • Example: “1998... sorry, I mean 1988”

For Spellings:

  • Write each letter carefully
  • Common in names and addresses
  • Example: “P-A-R-K-E-R”

Reflection

Ask yourself:

  • Did I predict the answer type before listening?
  • Did I focus on numbers and spellings?
  • Did I check word limits?
